About Us

MT Longonot Mabati Factory is an upcoming manufacturer of high quality roofing sheets. We provide reliable and durable roofing solutions to homes and businesses across Kenya. We are located in Mirera Mastima Centre along Naivasha–Mai Mahiu highway in Naivasha.

We manufacture a wide range of profiles — box profile, Longonot tile shapes, roll-top, bowtile, ridge caps, valley sheets and offer nails & rubbers. Our team focuses on durable finishes, consistent quality and reliable delivery.
